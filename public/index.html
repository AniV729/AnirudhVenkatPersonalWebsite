Quantum Randomness for Healthcare Informatics

Q# · Azure Quantum · IonQ · NIST 800-22

Developed a true quantum random number generator using Q# on Azure Quantum with execution on IonQ hardware. Applied the generator to healthcare data de-identification, validating randomness quality against the NIST 800-22 statistical test suite. Presented at ISEF, winning CIA 1st Place.

The Australian Shuffle & Two Self-Working Card Tricks

Published · The Mathematical Gazette · June 2025

A peer-reviewed mathematical analysis of the Australian (out-shuffle / Monge) shuffle and two self-working card tricks derived from its properties. The paper establishes formal proofs connecting shuffle cycle structure to predictable card positions, surfacing deep combinatorial patterns accessible through recreational mathematics.

CombinatoricsGroup TheoryMathematical Gazette

Exfiltrating Data from an Isolated System

Covert Channel · Screen Brightness Modulation · US Navy ONR Medallion

Demonstrated a novel covert data exfiltration channel from air-gapped systems by encoding information in screen brightness modulation — invisible to the human eye but detectable by a camera at distance. The attack bypasses traditional air-gap security without requiring physical media or network access. Presented at JSHS and awarded the US Navy ONR Medallion.

Covert ChannelsAir-Gap SecuritySignal Processing
