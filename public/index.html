A peer-reviewed mathematical analysis of the Australian (out-shuffle / Monge) shuffle and two self-working card tricks derived from its properties. Exfiltrating Data from an Isolated System

Covert Channel · Screen Brightness Modulation · US Navy ONR Medallion

Demonstrated a novel covert data exfiltration channel from air-gapped systems by encoding information in screen brightness modulation — invisible to the human eye but detectable by a camera at distance. The attack bypasses traditional air-gap security without requiring physical media or network access. Presented at JSHS and awarded the US Navy ONR Medallion.
